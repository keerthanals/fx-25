Welcome to EFEX Fest,
the ultimate celebration of
innovation, creativity, and camaraderie!
Organized by the students of the
Department of Mathematical Sciences of
St. Joseph’s College (Autonomous), Devagiri.
EFEX features a dynamic mix of
technical, mathematical,and non-technical
events designed to inspire and
challenge participants. From cutting-edge
tech competitions and problem-solving
math battles to fun-filled non-tech activities,
there’s something for everyone.
Whether you’re here to showcase your skills,
spark your curiosity, or simply enjoy
the camaraderie, EFEX promises
an exciting journey for all!
